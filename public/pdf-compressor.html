Add to iPhone Home Screen
1
Open this page in Safari — Chrome and Firefox on iOS don't support home screen apps.
2
Tap the Share button — the box-with-arrow icon at the bottom of the screen (or top-right in iPad).
3
Scroll the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Tap Add. The app opens full-screen from your home screen with no browser chrome, just like a native app.
⚠ The page must be served over https:// for this to work properly — host it on your Nextcloud or sajivfrancis.com. All processing runs locally in your browser; no files are uploaded anywhere.
